
### Chapter 5: Taxes on Gifts and Inheritances Summary
This chapter covers taxes triggered by the transfer of assets due to death or donation.

- **Gift Tax:**
    - Levied on individuals who acquire property through gifts during a calendar year.
    - Returns must be filed between **February 1 and March 15** of the following year.
    - For gifts received after January 1, 2003, taxpayers may opt for a special tax settlement at the time of inheritance under certain conditions.
- **Inheritance Tax:**
    - Levied on individuals who acquire property through inheritance or bequest.
    - Returns must be filed within **ten months** from the date of death (inheritance).
    - The scope of taxable property depends on whether the inheritor has an address (domicile) in Japan or outside Japan.

### Chapter 6: Tax on Consumption Summary
This chapter explains Japan's version of a sales tax (VAT).

- **Nature of the Tax:** Consumption tax is levied on the sale, lease of goods, and provision of services in Japan.
- **Who Pays:** Business enterprises are responsible for collecting and paying the tax. However, because the tax is added to the price of goods and services, the cost is ultimately borne by the **consumer**.
- **Scope:**
    - **Imported goods** are taxable.
    - **Exported products** are exempt from taxation.
- **Filing:** Individuals and corporations must file returns calculating the net amount of consumption tax owed (consumption tax received from customers minus consumption tax paid on their own business expenses).

### Chapter 7: Taxes on Properties and Transactions Summary
This chapter outlines various taxes imposed on the ownership, acquisition, or registration of assets and documents.

- **(1) Property Tax and City Planning Tax:**
    - **Fixed Property Tax:** Paid to the municipality by owners of real property (land, houses) and depreciable business assets (machinery, equipment).
    - **City Planning Tax:** Paid to the municipality by owners of land and houses to finance urban planning projects.
- **(2) Automobile Tax and Light Motor Vehicle Tax:**
    - **Automobile Tax:** Paid to the prefecture by car owners based on the vehicle's garage location.
    - **Light Motor Vehicle Tax:** Paid to the municipality by owners of motorbikes, light vehicles, and small scooters.
- **(3) Business Office Tax:** Paid by corporations and individuals doing business in specially designated areas (like Tokyo), based on office floor space and total employee salaries.
- **(4) Registration and License Tax:**
    - **Real Property Registration:** Paid when registering ownership of land or buildings.
    - **Commerce Registration:** Paid for company establishment, capital increases, branch registrations, changes in directors, and registration of patents or copyrights.
- **(5) Real Property Acquisition Tax:** Paid to the prefecture by corporations and individuals who acquire land or buildings.
- **(6) Stamp Tax:** Paid by affixing revenue stamps to documents evidencing economic transactions, such as contracts, securities, and receipts.
